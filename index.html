Software Freedom Day

Software Freedom Day (SFD) is an annual worldwide celebration of Free Software. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use. Every local community or GLUG would probably celebrate SFD in their locality and will interact with the society stating the importance of Free Software.This year we have lots more to do...
